Iceland!

Iceland is a country in the northwestern part of the Northern Hemisphere. It is located in the Arctic Ocean, and is the only country in the world to have a continent bordering the Arctic Ocean.

Explore Iceland's wonders in 4 days! Begin in Reykjavik, marveling at its vibrant culture. Visit the Golden Circle: Thingvellir National Park, Geysir, and Gullfoss Waterfall. Experience the South Coast with black sand beaches, Skogafoss, and Seljalandsfoss. Relax in the Blue Lagoon before departure. Perfect for nature lovers and adventurers!
